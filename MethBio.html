Transforming Cow Excrement into Clean Energy

MethBio is a startup that specializes in the production of biogas from cow excrement. Our unique technology allows us to extract clean energy from a plentiful and sustainable source, while also reducing the environmental impact of livestock farming.

About Us

MethBio was founded by a group of engineers and scientists who are passionate about creating sustainable solutions to the world's energy needs. Our team has decades of combined experience in the fields of biogas production, waste management, and environmental science.

Our Technology

At MethBio, we utilize a state-of-the-art anaerobic digestion process to break down cow excrement and other organic waste materials into biogas. Our technology is highly efficient, cost-effective, and scalable, making it an ideal solution for farms of all sizes.

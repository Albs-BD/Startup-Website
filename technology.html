Our Technology

Biogas Production

At MethBio, we use a process called anaerobic digestion to produce biogas from cow manure. Anaerobic digestion is a natural process in which microorganisms break down organic matter in the absence of oxygen. We use a specially designed digester to optimize this process and extract as much biogas as possible.

Biogas Upgrading

The biogas produced from the anaerobic digestion process contains impurities such as carbon dioxide and water vapor. In order to make the biogas usable as a fuel, we use a process called biogas upgrading to remove these impurities. This results in a high-quality biomethane fuel that can be used to power vehicles, heat buildings, and generate electricity.

Biogas Utilization

There are many ways to use biogas, depending on the specific needs of our customers. Some of the most common applications include:

  • Vehicle fuel: Biomethane can be used as a replacement for gasoline or diesel in vehicles.
  • Heat and power: Biomethane can be used to generate heat and electricity for homes, businesses, and farms.
  • Fertilizer: The leftover digestate from the anaerobic digestion process can be used as a nutrient-rich fertilizer for crops.
